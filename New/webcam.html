    Product Description
    Powerful performance combined with a user-friendly experience, the Sony ZV-E10 Camera is a must-have. To give your vlog exceptional and distinctive visuals, this camera allows you to select the right lens from Sony's extensive selection of E-mount lenses. In addition, a wide-angle lens can also be used if you want to include the surrounding landscape in your picture. Besides, you can use a fixed-focal-length lens to emphasise the subject while gradually blurring the background. Moreover, you can use a telephoto lens to focus on distant scenes and a macro lens for close-up images.
    Easy Lens Selection

    You can choose the appropriate lens from Sony's large collection of E-mount lenses for the Sony ZV-E10 Camera to boost your vlog with excellent and distinctive visuals. In addition, if you want to include the surrounding landscape in your narrative, you can use a wide-angle lens. And, if you want to draw attention to the subject while subtly blurring the background, you can use a fixed-focal-length lens. Besides, you can use a macro lens for closeup shots and a telephoto lens to bring distant scenes into focus.

    High-quality Images

    Featuring a large APS-C-sized image sensor, this camera delivers high-quality images. And, the image sensor is large enough, so it renders excellent, expert-looking photographs with high resolution and fine details.

    Efficient Lighting and Minimal Noise

    Even if you're shooting in low-light conditions, this camera's large-format image sensor maximises light collection and produces up to 4K movies with high-quality images and reduced noise.

    Rapid Control Button Access

    Equipped with a MOVIE button, which is located right on top, this camera ensures rapid access. It also features a convenient zoom lever and Still, Movie, and S&Q buttons. So, these buttons make it hassle-free to switch between still photos, regular videos, and Slow and Quick Motion modes.

    Easy Selfie Clicking and Vlogging

    As this camera boasts a lightweight design, you can take it along on the go when you're ready to vlog. Even when you are shooting from a low or high position, this camera's side-opening vari-angle LCD screen lets you conveniently click selfies and check your framing. Besides, thanks to its ergonomic grip, this camera ensures a secure and stable hold.

    Detailed Visuals

    Leveraging a full-pixel readout from the sensor, this camera's internal up to 4K video recordings can capture around 2.4 times as much data as 4K requires, which is roughly equivalent to 6K. Courtesy of this oversampling, the captured video gets more visual information compressed into each frame, producing exceptionally vivid video images.

    Rapid and Accurate Autofocus

    Even if it is moving quickly, this camera's Fast Hybrid AF technology keeps the subject in focus. This wide coverage and dense AF point arrangement, which together account for 425 phase-detection points and up to 84% of the image area of the sensor, enable the exact capture of objects that might otherwise be challenging to focus on.

    Ideal Focus Transitions

    By just pressing a button you can activate this camera's product showcase setting, which is ideal for product review videos. And, when this setting is activated, its focus automatically changes to the object when you hold a product up in front of this camera and returns to your face after the product has been moved out of the frame. This setting is available for use even when image stabilisation is turned on and set to Active.
